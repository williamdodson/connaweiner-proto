Those who work with Conna benefit from her diverse experience as a hand-on litigator and manager and strategic legal business, regulatory, transactional and compliance problem solver.

The Inside Edge

Conna has over 20 years of experience inside global corporations in the U.S. and abroad, including as a General Counsel. In 2013, Conna was awarded an ADR Higginbotham Fellowship by the American Arbitration Association.

Conna has:

  • In-the-trenches, practical experience with numerous types of challenging legal issues and contractual relationships in different geographies;
  • Experience with complex general commercial matters and extensive background in the highly regulated and technical life sciences/healthcare arena;
  • A sophisticated understanding of why and how internal and external deals and collaborations get done or fall apart;
  • The ability to listen fairly and well and cut through to the heart of a problem even in areas that are new territory;
  • An intimate knowledge of the true cost to companies and individuals—beyond mere time and money—of ill-advised conflict escalation, particularly litigation among business partners in an on-going relationship;
  • A vigorous project-management approach to litigation when it does happen;
  • An appreciation of diverse cultural perspective as a result of her international experience.

Conna can support companies and individuals across the U.S. and abroad, giving them fair, informed, customized, cost-effective dispute resolution services.
