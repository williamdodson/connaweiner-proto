Work History

Independent Consultant (part-time), Axiom, Inc., 2013-present; General Counsel, North America, Mylan, Inc., 2010-12; Research Consultant on Legislative Project (non-legal), Clark University, 2012; Vice President and Lead Counsel, Shire Human Genetics Therapy, 2009-10; U.S and global roles at Novartis from 1993 - 2009, including the following: Head Litigation, Ex-Americas, 2009; General Counsel and Global Compliance Officer, Novartis Animal Health, Inc./Novartis, 2007-09; Vice President, Legal Novartis Pharmaceuticals Corporation, 2004-2007; Executive Director and Section Head approximately 1996 on; Litigation Associate, Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison, 1987-93.

Experience

Attorney and compliance professional with diversified U.S. and global business counseling, transactional, litigation and management experience, first as a civil litigator at a law firm and then during almost twenty years of in-house research, development, commercial, transactional (licensing, mergers and acquisitions, supply/manufacturing, etc.) regulatory, intellectual property and litigation experience in the life sciences, primarily in the pharmaceutical industry (human and animal branded and generic small molecule and biotech therapeutics and, on occasion, associated devices and vaccines).

Civil litigation at Paul, Weiss, Rifkind Wharton & Garrison included securities, SEC investigation, Lanham Act/trademarks, loan agreements, contract purchase price adjustments and significant arbitration (see below).

U.S.-based experience at Ciba-Geigy/Novartis, Shire and/or Mylan included general commercial and corporate contracting and issues, life sciences/healthcare-specific complex collaborations and transactions with a variety of parties, including life sciences companies, insurers, an academic medical center, etc. (domestic and global co-development/co-commercialization agreements, licensing, supply/distribution, mergers and acquisitions, divestments (voluntary and government-required) postmerger/acquisition integration planning), research and development counseling/drafting in connection with global clinical trials, clinical research organization arrangements, numerous complex commercial and regulatory questions including intellectual property, product launch, advertising and promotion, antikickback, FCPA, privacy, Pharma/OIG compliance guidance policy development, training, investigations, etc., customer purchase and distribution contracting (including with retailers, wholesalers, hospitals and hospital buying groups, specialty pharmacy, managed care organizations), pricing and reimbursement questions (e.g, federal government contracting, Medicare and Medicaid, often requiring understanding of customer reimbursement issues such as hospitals (DRG's) and managed care organizations), antitrust (general counseling and significant litigation, including In Re Brand Name Prescription Drug Litigation, multiple individual and class action Robinson-Patman and Sherman Act cases) securities law and disclosures, corporate governance questions, Hatch-Waxman patent litigation and related regulatory questions, trade association counseling and legislative review, Latin-American compliance/legal investigations.

Switzerland-based experience included most of the above plus global patent and trademark department management as part of responsibilities as General Counsel and Global Compliance officer for Novartis Animal Health, training and experience as required by issues arising in practice with local country laws outside the U.S. and European Union laws/directives (such as competition law, privacy), and direct global compliance officer experience and Ex-Americas global litigation management experience, including Sarbanes-Oxley-related issues.
