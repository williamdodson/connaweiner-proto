Mediation & Arbitration Services

A unique inside perspective on alternative dispute resolution

Conna Weiner brings a unique inside perspective on alternative dispute resolution. Those who work with Conna benefit from her diverse experience as a hands-on litigator, as well as her experience as a strategic legal business, litigation, regulatory, transactional and compliance problem solver.

Many arbitrators and mediators were in the judiciary or were outside litigation counsel. Conna has over 20 years of experience inside global corporations in the U.S. and abroad, including as a General Counsel.

The Benefit of Experience

Conna’s clients benefit from her experience in the following ways:

  • In-the-trenches, practical experience with numerous types of challenging legal issues and contractual relationships in different geographies.
  • Experience with complex general commercial matters and specific experience in the highly regulated and technical life sciences/healthcare arena;
  • Sophisticated understanding of why and how internal and external deals and collaborations get done or fall apart;
  • Ability to listen fairly and well and cut through to the heart of a problem even in areas that are new territory;
  • Intimate knowledge of the true cost to companies and individuals—beyond mere time and money—of ill-advised conflict escalation, particularly litigation among business partners in an on-going relationship;
  • Application of a vigorous, project-management approach to litigation when it does happen;
  • Cultural sensitivity to different ways of thinking and accomplishing goals.

Conna can support companies and individuals across the U.S. and abroad, giving them fair, informed, customized, cost-effective dispute resolution services.
